欢迎使用 LunaCycle（以下简称"我们"或"本应用"）。本隐私政策说明我们在你使用本应用时，会收集哪些信息、为什么收集、如何使用与保护，以及你可以如何行使自己的权利。请在使用本应用前仔细阅读本政策。

1. 我们收集的信息

账户信息：如果你选择使用 Google 登录或 Apple 登录，我们会获取你授权提供的邮箱地址、姓名与头像，用于创建和识别你的账户。如果你不登录，仅在本机使用，我们不会收集此类信息。

健康与周期数据：你在应用中记录的经期、排卵、症状、心情、睡眠、体温、体重、运动、饮水、亲密关系、用药、备注等信息。这些数据默认只保存在你设备本地的数据库中，不会自动上传。

云备份数据（可选功能）：如果你登录账户并主动开启"云备份"，上述健康与周期数据的备份会上传并存储在由 Google Firebase 提供的 Cloud Firestore 中，以便你更换设备时可以恢复数据。你可以随时在设置中关闭该功能，并删除已上传的云端备份。

使用与分析数据：我们使用 Firebase Analytics 收集应用使用情况，例如功能使用频率、页面访问、设备型号、操作系统版本与崩溃日志，用于排查问题和改进产品体验。

广告数据：我们使用 Google AdMob 在应用中展示广告，可能会用到广告标识符（如 iOS 的 IDFA）以投放更相关的广告。在 iOS 设备上，我们会依据系统的 App 跟踪透明度（ATT）框架请求你的授权，仅在你同意后才会用于跨应用跟踪；你可以随时在系统设置中修改此授权。

购买信息：如果你购买 VIP 会员或其他内购项目，交易由 Apple App Store 或 Google Play 处理，我们不会获取你的银行卡号等支付信息，只会收到购买状态用于为你解锁相应权益。

2. 我们如何使用这些信息

  • 提供周期记录、预测与图表统计等核心功能；
  • 在你开启云备份时，实现跨设备的数据同步与恢复；
  • 分析并改进应用性能与用户体验，排查故障；
  • 展示广告，并在你授权的情况下进行个性化投放；
  • 处理订阅与内购，验证并发放会员权益；
  • 回应你的咨询与反馈。

3. 数据存储与安全

你的健康与周期数据默认保存在设备本地的数据库中，并受你设备自身的系统级安全机制保护。若你开启云备份，数据在传输过程中通过 HTTPS/TLS 加密，云端数据存储在 Google Firebase 的基础设施中。我们采取合理的技术与管理措施保护你的信息，但请注意任何互联网传输或存储方式都无法做到绝对安全。

4. 第三方服务

本应用集成了以下第三方服务，它们可能会按照各自的隐私政策独立处理数据：

  • Firebase Authentication / Cloud Firestore / Firebase Analytics（Google）
  • Google 登录（Google Sign-In）
  • Apple 登录（Sign in with Apple）
  • Google AdMob（广告）
  • Apple App Store / Google Play 内购结算

建议同时查阅这些第三方的隐私政策，以了解它们各自的数据处理方式。

5. 你的选择与权利

  • 你可以随时在设置中关闭云备份，并删除已上传的云端备份数据；
  • 你可以在系统设置中关闭个性化广告跟踪（iOS 的"跟踪"权限、Android 的广告个性化设置）；
  • 你可以卸载应用以删除设备本地保存的全部数据；
  • 如你所在地区法律（如 GDPR、CCPA）赋予你访问、更正、删除、导出个人信息等权利，你可以通过下方联系方式向我们提出请求，我们会在合理时间内处理。

6. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 周岁（或你所在地区法律规定的最低年龄）以下的儿童，我们不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。如果我们发现在不知情的情况下收集了儿童信息，将尽快删除。

7. 数据保留

本地数据会保留至你手动删除记录或卸载应用；云备份数据会保留至你删除备份或注销账户。

8. 跨境数据传输

由于我们使用 Google Firebase 等全球基础设施，你的数据（在你开启云备份的情况下）可能会被传输、存储或处理于你所在国家/地区以外的服务器上。

9. 政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本政策。如有重大变更，我们会在应用内或本页面进行提示。建议你定期查看本页面以了解最新内容。

Welcome to LunaCycle ("we", "us", or "the app"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the app, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and how you can exercise your rights. Please read this policy carefully before using the app.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: If you choose to sign in with Google or Apple, we receive the email address, name, and profile photo you authorize us to access, used to create and identify your account. If you use the app without signing in, we do not collect this information.

Health & cycle data: Period, ovulation, symptoms, mood, sleep, temperature, weight, exercise, water intake, intimacy, medication, and notes that you log in the app. This data is stored locally on your device by default and is not uploaded automatically.

Cloud backup data (optional): If you sign in and actively enable "Cloud Backup," a backup of the health and cycle data above is uploaded to and stored in Google Firebase Cloud Firestore so you can restore it when switching devices. You can disable this feature at any time in Settings and delete any backups already uploaded.

Usage & analytics data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect app usage information such as feature usage frequency, screen views, device model, OS version, and crash logs, to diagnose issues and improve the product.

Advertising data: We use Google AdMob to display ads, which may use advertising identifiers (such as IDFA on iOS) to serve more relevant ads. On iOS, we request your authorization via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework and only track you across apps if you consent; you can change this permission at any time in system settings.

Purchase information: If you purchase a VIP subscription or other in-app item, the transaction is processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play. We never receive your card number or other payment details — only a purchase status used to unlock the corresponding benefits.

2. How We Use This Information

  • To provide core features such as cycle logging, prediction, and charts;
  • To sync and restore your data across devices when Cloud Backup is enabled;
  • To analyze and improve app performance and user experience, and to diagnose issues;
  • To display ads, and to personalize them where you have consented;
  • To process subscriptions and in-app purchases and grant the corresponding benefits;
  • To respond to your inquiries and feedback.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your health and cycle data is stored locally on your device by default and protected by your device's own system-level security mechanisms. If you enable Cloud Backup, data is encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS, and cloud data is stored on Google Firebase's infrastructure. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage over the internet is ever completely secure.

4. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party services, which may independently process data under their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Authentication / Cloud Firestore / Firebase Analytics (Google)
  • Google Sign-In
  • Sign in with Apple
  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play billing

We recommend also reviewing these third parties' own privacy policies to understand how each of them handles data.

5. Your Choices & Rights

  • You can disable Cloud Backup at any time in Settings and delete any backups already uploaded;
  • You can turn off personalized ad tracking in system settings (iOS "Tracking" permission, Android ad personalization settings);
  • You can uninstall the app to delete all data stored locally on your device;
  • Where applicable law (such as GDPR or CCPA) grants you rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information, you may contact us using the details below and we will respond within a reasonable time.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required by your local law), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have inadvertently collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Retention

Local data is retained until you delete the entries or uninstall the app; cloud backup data is retained until you delete the backup or delete your account.

8. International Data Transfers

Because we rely on global infrastructure such as Google Firebase, your data (when Cloud Backup is enabled) may be transferred, stored, or processed on servers located outside your country or region.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you in the app or on this page. Please check back periodically for the latest version.
